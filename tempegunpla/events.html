Events

Build Nights

Build nights happen every fourth Sunday of the month. We gather at Hobby Action in tempe. There are tables available for you to build on. There are specials on build not kits. Build the same kit as the group or one of your. Come see what others are working. Ask the more experienced builders questions or just hang out. There are different tools for you to try out. Want to learn how to airbrush your model, build night has it available, along with other more advanced tools.Bring in any model kits that is giving you trouble. Hobby Action also offers 10% off kits, so if there is a kit you have been waiting to get Build Night is the best time to buy. Looking for a model? Or have a backlog of kits? Come trade on build night.

Contest

We host two different kinds of contests. 1st is the build night contest. At the beginning of the month we select a build theme. Then there are three levels of builds, Expert , Advance, and Beginner. Each level has limits that can be done. These will be posted with the theme at the beginning of the month. Hobby Action has prizes for the winners. This is a great opportunity to get feedback on your skills.
